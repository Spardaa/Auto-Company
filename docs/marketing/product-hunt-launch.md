## 3. First Comment (Maker Story)

**The Story:**

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

Two years into running my SaaS, I noticed a pattern. Every month, I'd open the same spreadsheets. Run the same calculations. Check the same benchmarks.

"What's my LTV:CAC ratio again?"
"How many months of runway do I have?"
"Am I hitting the Rule of 40?"

I looked for tools to help. But everything wanted my email, my credit card, or my firstborn child.

So I built what I wished existed.

**Spardaa started as one calculator.** Then two. Then ten. Now it's 30 tools that I actually use:

- The **SaaS Scorecard** replaced my monthly spreadsheet ritual
- The **Cohort Analysis** helped me spot churn before it spiraled
- The **JSON Formatter** became my daily driver
- The **Color Palette** tool saved me hours on landing pages

**Why free?**

Three reasons:

1. **Permission over interruption.** I'd rather earn your trust than buy your attention.

2. **Privacy matters.** Your metrics are nobody's business. Everything runs in your browser - no server, no database, no tracking.

3. **Tools should be tools.** Not lead magnets. Not funnels. Just useful things that work.

**What's next?**

More tools based on what you need. Drop a comment with your wish list - I read every one.

Thanks for checking it out. If you find it useful, tell one founder friend. That's how this grows.
