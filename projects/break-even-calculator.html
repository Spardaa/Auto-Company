🚀 Strategies to Reach Break-Even Faster

1. Increase Prices

A 20% price increase reduces your break-even point by 23%. If your value proposition supports it, this is the fastest path to profitability.

2. Reduce Variable Costs

Negotiate better hosting rates, optimize cloud usage, or automate support. Every dollar saved per customer increases contribution margin.

3. Cut Fixed Costs

Audit your subscriptions, consider remote work to reduce office costs, and delay non-essential hires until after break-even.

4. Accelerate Growth

Invest in high-ROI acquisition channels. Each month you reach break-even faster saves you $10,000 in burn.

5. Improve Retention

Lower churn means each customer is worth more, effectively reducing your break-even point over time.

6. Add Upsells

Increase ARPC with add-ons, premium tiers, or annual plans. Higher ARPC = fewer customers needed to break even.
